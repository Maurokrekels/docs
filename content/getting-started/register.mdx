# Create Account

import { Callout } from "nextra/components"

FiveGateway uses **Discord OAuth** as its only login method. You need a Discord account to get started.

## Steps

1. Go to [my.fivegateway.com](https://my.fivegateway.com).
2. Click **Login with Discord**.
3. Authorize FiveGateway to read your Discord profile.
4. You will be redirected to the dashboard where you can immediately add a server.

<Callout type="info">
    Your Discord account is your identity within FiveGateway. All servers and
    settings are linked to your Discord account.
</Callout>

## Choosing a Plan

After creating your account you automatically start on the **Starter** plan (free, 1 server). You can upgrade to Pro or Premium at any time via the settings in the dashboard.

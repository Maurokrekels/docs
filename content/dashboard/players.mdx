# Players

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

The players section gives you insight into who is playing on your server and who has played in the past.

## Online Players

The online players table shows in real-time who is on your server, including:

- **Name** — The in-game name of the player.
- **Server ID** — The current server ID.
- **Identifiers** — Discord, license, Steam and other identifiers.
- **Ping** — The player's current ping.
- **Playtime** — How long the player has been online in the current session.

## Searching Players

Use the search bar to find players by name, identifier or server ID. This works for both online and offline players.

<Callout type="info">
  The search function searches all historical data. You can find players who are no longer online.
</Callout>

## Player Profile

Click on a player to view their full profile:

- **Session History** — Overview of all sessions with join and leave times.
- **Total Playtime** — Total playtime across all sessions.
- **Identifiers** — All known identifiers for the player.
- **Related Logs** — Logs that are linked to this player.
- **Fingerprint** — A unique browser fingerprint used for identifying alt accounts.

## Data Retention

How long player data is kept depends on your plan. Check your plan settings for the exact retention period.
